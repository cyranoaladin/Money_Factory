Problem & Vision

Market Problem

The Web3 / Solana ecosystem suffers from a fragmented and inefficient venture formation process:

  • Builders must navigate 50+ tools, 20+ protocols, and inconsistent documentation
  • Capital access remains opaque, slow and network-dependent (6–12 month fundraising cycles)
  • No coherent pipeline exists from idea to launch with technical and governance support
  • In practice, a majority of projects fail before the mainnet, by execution friction rather than lack of merit

Vision

Money Factory AI aims to become the "production layer" of the Internet Capital Market on Solana:

  • An AI co-founder for every builder, orchestrating 17 specialized agents
  • A structured journey of education, construction, governance and launch
  • A hub of incubation where the DAO filters, funds and supports the most solid projects
  • A programmatic portfolio of graduated protocols that sustainably feed the treasury and $MFAI holders

Product & Platform

4-Phase Journey

  • Phase 1 – Learn & Earn: Modules, missions, earn $MFAI and Proof-of-Skill NFTs
  • Phase 2 – Build with Zyno: Describe your project, Zyno produces Project Canvas
  • Phase 3 – DAO Approval: Submit to DAO with voting power based on stake + skills
  • Phase 4 – Incubation & Launch: 8–12 week program with Collaterize bonding curves

Zyno – Multi-Agent Orchestration Kernel

Zyno is the multi-agent orchestration kernel that powers both AEPO and AECO:

  • AEPO (AI-Enhanced Pathway Orchestration) – Orchestrates individual learning and building paths
  • AECO (AI-Enhanced Cohort Orchestration) – Orchestrates group dynamics and cohort programs
  • Routes tasks to 17 specialized agents (Architect, Engineer, CFO, etc.)
  • Accesses RAG knowledge base (Solana/Web3 documentation)
  • Synthesizes responses into UI blocks and actionable artifacts

Business Model & Revenues

Off-Chain Revenues – SaaS (Zyno Pro)

Subscription plans:

  • Free – Learning modules, limited Zyno, 1 project slot
  • Builder – $49 / month – Unlimited Zyno, 5 project slots, advanced agents
  • Pro – $149 / month – Everything Builder + custom journeys, white-label options
  • Enterprise – Custom pricing – Dedicated agents, on-premise, specific integrations

On-Chain Revenues – Launchpad, Bonding Curves, DAO

For each graduated project:

  • Fixed Launch Fee – typically $25k (USDC or $MFAI)
  • Success Fee – 2–5% of raise (in project tokens, vested)
  • Bonding Curve Fees – 1–3% per trade (shared: treasury, creators, LPs)
  • DAO Governance Revenues – potential revenue shares (5–10%), appreciation of tokens held by treasury

Risks & Mitigation Strategy

  • Technical Risks: Multi-agent systems have complexity; we mitigate with extensive testing and modular architecture.
