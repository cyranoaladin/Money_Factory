Interacting with Zyno AI

Zyno is designed to be intuitive and helpful. You can interact with Zyno through the chat interface in the bottom right corner or through the dedicated Terminal page.

DAO Governance

Money Factory AI is governed by its community. The DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) ensures that decisions are made transparently and democratically.

Propose

Any member with sufficient voting power can submit a proposal.

Vote

Members vote on proposals using their staked $MFAI and reputation.

Execute

Approved proposals are automatically executed by smart contracts.

Tokenomics

The $MFAI token captures the value created by the ecosystem. It is used for governance, staking, and accessing premium features.

Utility Description
Governance Vote on DAO proposals and project funding.
Staking Earn yield and increase voting power.
Access Unlock Zyno Pro features and advanced analytics.
