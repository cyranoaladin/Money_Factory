Credits & Access Model

  • Zyno Credits:

    Every interaction with an AI agent consumes credits. Simple Q&A costs less; generating a full smart contract audit costs more. Users can top up credits using $MFAI or SOL.

  • Builder Tier:

    Holders of the Builder Pass or sufficient $MFAI stake utilize a higher tier, unlocking advanced agents (e.g., Senior Architect, Legal Compliance) and faster process times.

Sample Use Cases

Solo Founder (AEPO)

Goal: Launch a DeFi prediction market.

User interacts with Zyno to define the idea. AEPO generates a 4-week roadmap. Week 1: Market research agent validates demand. Week 2: Tokenomics agent models the economy. Week 3: Engineer agent codes the MVP.

DAO Team (AECO)

Goal: Govern a community treasury.

AECO sets up a shared dashboard. Zyno facilitates weekly governance calls, summarizes proposals, and tracks voting deadlines. The "CFO Agent" provides monthly treasury reports to all members automatically.

DAO & Liquidity

DAO Governance

Money Factory AI is governed by its community. The DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) ensures that decisions are made transparently and democratically.

Propose

Any member with sufficient voting power can submit a proposal.

Vote

Members vote on proposals using their staked $MFAI and reputation.

Execute

Approved proposals are automatically executed by smart contracts.

Tokenomics

The $MFAI token captures the value created by the ecosystem. It is used for simulated governance, staking, and accessing premium features.
