Everything you need to know about Money Factory AI

Do I need to know how to code to use Money Factory AI?
No, absolutely not. Zyno and its 17 AI agents handle all the code for you. Your role is to bring the Vision, Strategy, and Creativity. If you can describe it, Zyno can architect and deploy it on Solana.
What are Proof-of-Skill‚Ñ¢ NFTs?
These are soulbound tokens (non-transferable) that verify your expertise on-chain. They serve as your Web3 resume and increase your voting weight in Synaptic‚Ñ¢ DAO governance. The more you learn and contribute, the greater your influence grows.
What's inside the Zyno Terminal?
The Terminal is your command center. It includes:
  • Chat interface with the Agentic Swarm
  • Real-time project dashboard
  • Learn & Earn training modules
  • Wallet and asset management
  • DAO voting portal
  • One-click contract deployment
How does the Collaterize integration work?
Every DAO-approved project is automatically deployed on Collaterize with a configured bonding curve. This ensures instant liquidity, transparent price discovery, and liquidity safety. Your tokens trade 24/7 from launch.
How much does launching a project cost?
$0 to start. The Learn & Earn pathway is free and rewards you in $MFAI. To deploy a DAO-validated project, minimal Solana network fees apply (~0.01-0.1 SOL). A small percentage (5%) of funds raised via bonding curve feeds the MFAI treasury.
How does DAO voting work?
Synaptic Governance‚Ñ¢ uses a weighted voting system based on:
  • $MFAI Staking: Stake more to increase your voting power
  • Proof-of-Skill‚Ñ¢ NFTs: Verified skills amplify your influence
  • On-Chain Reputation: Track record of contributions and successful projects

The best projects are selected by merit, not capital alone.
