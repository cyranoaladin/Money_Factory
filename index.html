No, absolutely not. Zyno and its 24 AI agents handle all the code for you. Your role is to bring the Vision, Strategy, and Creativity. If you can describe it, Zyno can architect and deploy it on Solana.

The Terminal is your command center. It includes:

How does the Collaterize integration work?
Every DAO-approved project is automatically deployed on Collaterize with a configured bonding curve. This ensures instant liquidity, transparent price discovery, and liquidity safety. Your tokens trade 24/7 from launch.

$0 to start. The Learn & Earn pathway is free and rewards you in $MFAI. To deploy a DAO-validated project, minimal Solana network fees apply (~0.01-0.1 SOL). A small percentage (5%) of funds raised via bonding curve feeds the MFAI treasury.
