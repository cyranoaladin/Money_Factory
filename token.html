Value Capture Engine

The ecosystem uses platform revenue to systematically reduce supply and reward holders.

The "Ignition" Protocol (Burn)

  • 25% of Credit Usage: Whenever Zyno Credits are consumed, 25% of the $MFAI value is permanently burned.
  • NFT Minting: 10% of fees from Proof-of-Skill NFT mints are burned.
  • Deflationary Spiral: As platform usage grows, supply decreases, increasing scarcity.

Platform Buybacks

  • Launchpad Revenue: 20% of success fees from incubated projects are used to buy back $MFAI from the open market.
  • SaaS Revenue: A portion of fiat/USDC subscriptions is converted to $MFAI for treasury reserves.
  • Treasury Growth: Buybacks deepen liquidity and support long-term price stability.
