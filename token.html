Synaptic Governance

Synaptic Governance is a merit-based system that rewards skills, stake, and participation to prevent plutocracy and ensure builders have a voice.

Voting Power Components

Staked $MFAI

Direct economic alignment (1:1 ratio)

Proof-of-Skill NFTs

Proven competence multiplier (√ó50 per NFT)

Historical Participation

Long-term engagement reward (√ó0.5 score)

Voting Mechanics

  • Voting Period: 3 days after 7-day review
  • Quorum: 10% of circulating $MFAI must vote
  • Approval Threshold: 60% YES votes required
  • Execution Timelock: 48 hours after approval (security buffer)

Guardrails Against Governance Attacks

  • Delegation Limits: Max 10% of total VP can be delegated to one address
  • Proposal Cooldown: 7 days between proposals from same address
  • Emergency Pause: 5/9 multi-sig can pause protocol in critical scenarios
  • Sybil Resistance: Proof-of-Skill NFTs are soulbound (non-transferable)

Proof-of-Skill NFTs

Proof-of-Skill NFTs are on-chain credentials that verify expertise. They are soulbound (non-transferable by default) and add +50 voting power each in Synaptic Governance.

Technical Specification

  • Standard: Metaplex (Solana NFT)
  • Metadata: On-chain + IPFS (images, descriptions, skill proofs)
  • Soulbound: Non-transferable by default (governance can enable trading)
  • Governance Weight: Each NFT adds +50 voting power

Tiered System (Planned)

  • Bronze (basic skills): 1.0x multiplier
  • Silver (intermediate): 1.5x multiplier
  • Gold (advanced): 2.0x multiplier
