How AEPO Works

  • Personalized Assessment: Zyno analyzes your skills, goals, and project idea
  • Dynamic Path Generation: AI creates a custom roadmap with milestones and tasks
  • Continuous Guidance: 24 specialized agents provide support at each phase
  • Proof-of-Skill™: Earn verifiable credentials and $MFAI as you progress

AECO

AI-Enhanced Cohort Orchestration

AECO structures teams & groups into synchronized progress, pairing stepping stones with collective milestones and peer+agent feedback loops.

Cohort Programs

Launch 8–12-week programs with synchronized milestones, group sessions, and peer reviews. Perfect for accelerators and educational initiatives.

  • Synchronized schedules
  • Group workshops & sessions
  • Peer feedback loops

Cross-User Dynamics

AI analyzes group interactions, identifies collaboration opportunities, and facilitates knowledge sharing between participants.

  • Smart team matching
  • Skill complementarity
  • Collaborative projects

Shared Milestones

Define collective goals and track progress across the entire cohort. Celebrate wins together and maintain momentum.

  • Group objectives
  • Progress dashboards
  • Collective achievements

Agent-Guided Sessions

Zyno agents facilitate group workshops, provide real-time feedback, and ensure every participant gets the support they need.

  • Live AI facilitation
  • Real-time Q&A
  • Personalized support
