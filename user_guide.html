Vision & Mission

The Problem

The Web3 venture formation process is fragmented, inefficient, and opaque. Builders struggle with tooling overload, while investors face high risks and slow capital deployment.

Money Factory AI aims to be the "Production Layer" of the Internet Capital Market on Solana. We provide:

  • → An AI Co-Founder for every builder (Zyno).
  • → A Structured Journey from idea to liquidity.
  • → A Meritocratic DAO that filters and funds the best projects.

The 4-Phase Journey

Phase 1: Learn & Earn

Users complete educational modules on Solana, DeFi, and Web3. They earn $MFAI tokens and Proof-of-Skill NFTs for their achievements.

Phase 2: Build with Zyno

Builders interact with Zyno to define their project. Zyno generates a "Project Canvas" – a comprehensive blueprint including technical specs, tokenomics, and roadmap.

Phase 3: DAO Approval

Projects are submitted to the DAO. Stakers and experts (NFT holders) vote on which projects to incubate. Approved projects receive initial support.

Phase 4: Launch & Liquidity

Projects undergo an incubation period and then launch via Collaterize bonding curves, ensuring instant liquidity and fair price discovery.

Zyno: The AI Engine

Zyno is the multi-agent orchestration kernel at the heart of Money Factory AI. It coordinates 17 specialized agents to assist builders.

AEPO

AI-Enhanced Pathway Orchestration. Guides individual users through personalized learning and building paths.

AECO

AI-Enhanced Cohort Orchestration. Manages group dynamics, hackathons, and cohort-based programs.

Governance

Money Factory AI uses Synaptic Governance, a merit-based system. Voting power is derived from:

  • Staked $MFAI (1:1 ratio)
  • Proof-of-Skill NFTs (Multiplier based on expertise)
  • Participation Score (Reward for active engagement)
