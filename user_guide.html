Governance Basics: Synaptic Governance

How voting power works and how to participate in DAO decisions

4.1 Voting Power Calculation

Your voting power in Synaptic Governance is calculated using three components:

Voting Power = (Staked $MFAI × 1.0) +
               (Your Proof-of-Skill NFTs × 50) +
               (Your Historical Participation × 0.5)

Example Calculation:

  • You stake 10,000 $MFAI → 10,000 VP
  • You have 3 Proof-of-Skill NFTs → 150 VP
  • Your participation score is 200 → 100 VP
  • Total Voting Power: 10,250

Why This Matters:

  • Pure token holders have power, but skilled contributors have proportionally more
  • Active participants who learn and engage get rewarded
  • Long-term alignment is incentivized over pure speculation

4.2 How to Vote on Project Incubations

01

Review Period

7 days
Read the Zyno dossier, check team credentials, review community comments, and use the evaluation criteria from Section 3.2.

02

Voting Window

3 days
Cast your vote: YES (approve for incubation) or NO (reject). Your vote is weighted by your Voting Power. You can change your vote during the window.

03

Results

Quorum: 10% of circulating $MFAI must participate
Approval: 60% YES votes needed
Results are on-chain and transparent.

04

Execution

Approved projects enter incubation (8-12 weeks). Treasury allocates resources. You can track incubation progress via public updates.

4.3 Proposing Improvements

Any holder with 10,000+ $MFAI can submit proposals.

Proposal Types:

Type Description Examples
Parameter Changes Adjust fees, thresholds, vesting schedules Change launchpad fee from 2% to 1.5%
Treasury Allocations Grants, partnerships, marketing budgets Allocate 50,000 $MFAI for university partnership
Protocol Upgrades Smart contract changes, new features Add quadratic voting option

Proposal Requirements:

  • Clear title and summary

Guardrails:

  • 7-day cooldown between proposals from same address
  • 48-hour timelock on approved proposals before execution
  • Multi-sig (5/9) can pause protocol in emergencies
  • Max 10% delegation to prevent centralization

4.4 Delegation

You can delegate your voting power to trusted community members:

  • Maximum 10% of total VP can be delegated to one entity (prevents centralization)
  • Delegation is revocable at any time
  • Delegates cannot move your tokens, only vote

Launchpad Participation

How to participate in project launches and understand Collaterize bonding curves

5.1 Launchpad Phases

Phase Duration Activities
Pre-Launch Weeks 1-12 Project is in incubation; weekly progress updates posted; community provides feedback; security audits conducted; marketing materials prepared
Launch Week 7 days Bonding curve contract deployed; initial parameters set by DAO; Token Generation Event (TGE) occurs; DEX listings go live (Raydium, Orca); trading begins immediately
Post-Launch Ongoing Community monitors metrics (TVL, users, volume); quarterly reports from project teams; DAO can propose parameter adjustments; long-term governance transitions to project's own DAO

5.2 Collaterize Parameters

When a project launches via Collaterize, key parameters include:

Reserve Ratio (0.2 - 0.5)

  • Lower ratio = steeper price curve
  • Higher ratio = more stable pricing
  • Typical: 0.3 - 0.4 for most projects

Initial Reserve (USDC)

  • Set based on project size and goals
  • Range: 50,000 - 500,000 USDC
  • Locked in smart contract (no admin withdrawal)

Fee Structure (1-3%)

  • Applied on buys and sells
  • Allocated to: Treasury (60%), Creator (20%), LP incentives (20%)
  • DAO can adjust fees via governance

Example Launch:

Reserve: 200,000 USDC
Ratio: 0.35
Fee: 2% (1.2% treasury, 0.4% creator, 0.4% LPs)
Initial Token Supply: 1,000,000
Starting Price: ~$0.63 per token

Price Discovery:

  • As users buy, reserve increases and supply increases → price rises algorithmically
  • As users sell, reserve decreases and supply decreases → price falls algorithmically

Rug-Pull Prevention:

  • ✅ Reserve is locked in contract
  • ✅ No admin keys to withdraw liquidity
  • ✅ Mathematical price discovery (no manipulation)
  • ✅ Transparent on-chain reserves
