Glossary of Terms

Staking (Simulated)
Locking Testnet $MFAI to access builder tools and governance rights on the Testnet.
Token ($MFAI)
The utility token of Money Factory AI (currently on Solana Devnet/Testnet).
AEPO
Automated Entity Public Offering. A solo founder pathway where Zyno agents act as your dedicated co-founders.
AECO
Automated Entity Cohort Offering. A collaborative team mode where Zyno orchestrates dynamics between multiple humans and agents.
Signal Score
A dynamic quality metric (0-100) assigned to projects based on code audits, roadmap progress, and community traction.
DAO Proposal
A simulated governance request submitted to MFAI holders to unlock Testnet funding or change protocol parameters.
Proof-of-Skill
NFT-based credentials verifying a user's on-chain contributions and expertise within the ecosystem.
Launchpad
The final stage where validated projects deploy their token and liquidity pools on Solana (Testnet).
Synaptic Governance
The DAO decision-making framework weighted by Testnet token stake and activity (Signal Score).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between AEPO and AECO?

AEPO is designed for solo founders or singular projects, providing dedicated AI agents. AECO is for cohorts/teams, emphasizing collaboration and shared milestones.

Where can I see the roadmap?

The roadmap is detailed on our Roadmap page. We update it quarterly based on DAO governance votes.

What is a Zyno Credit?

1 Zyno Credit (Testnet) ≈ $0.50 USD (Simulated). Credits are used to pay AI agents for specific deliverables like generating white papers, auditing smart contracts, or modeling tokenomics.
